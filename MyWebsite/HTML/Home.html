Welcome to my website, where I showcase my professional experience and skills as an audio technician and IT student. I am thrilled to have this platform to highlight my expertise in the audio and technology industry. My goal is to provide potential employers and clients with a comprehensive overview of my qualifications and work history, as well as to share my knowledge and thoughts on the field of audio production and its intersection with information technology.

TechProAV targets individuals and companies in the IT and AV industry, including IT technicians, AV technicians, event planners, and audio/video production companies. The audience for the website is diverse in terms of age, gender, and socioeconomic class, with a shared interest in IT and AV technologies.

The website includes a variety of content, including text describing my skills and experience, photos, videos, and audio samples showcasing my work. There is also a section for customer testimonials, and a contact form for potential employers to reach out to me. Additionally, links to my social media are available on the website.

Projects

As a seasoned theatre technician, I have provided sound design services for numerous productions, including both musicals and plays. My experience ranges from intimate black box theaters to large-scale productions on grand stages. I have a keen ear for creating soundscapes that perfectly complement the performances on stage, and I'm skilled in using industry-standard software to produce high-quality sound.

With years of experience working as a live sound technician, I have gained the expertise required to deliver high-quality sound at any type of event. I have worked on concerts, music festivals, corporate events, and more, and I always strive to ensure that the sound is crystal clear and perfectly balanced. Whether you need me to mix sound for a small band or a large orchestra, I have the skills and experience required to deliver exceptional results.

When KingdomworK Studio, a local recording studio, was destroyed by a flood, I was brought in to help rebuild the systems from the ground up. I collaborated with other technicians to design and build a state-of-the-art recording system that features the latest technology and equipment. Today, KingdomworK Studio is a thriving recording space that has helped to produce excellent music.

In my free time, I enjoy working on my own audio projects in my home studio. I use my skills and knowledge to produce high-quality recordings that sound professional and polished. My home studio is equipped with the latest technology, including industry-standard microphones and digital audio workstations. Whether I'm producing music or recording voiceovers, I always strive to achieve the highest quality sound possible.

As an IT student, I have worked on several projects that have allowed me to apply my technical skills and knowledge in a practical way. For example, I worked on a project that involved developing a new website for a local business. I also worked on a project that involved setting up a new computer network for a non-profit organization. These projects have given me the opportunity to hone my skills in web development, network administration, and other areas of IT.
