KingdomworK

LWorking on the Kingdomwork project has been both challenging and rewarding. After repairing and reconfiguring the recording systems, I am now focused on developing a remote access solution that would allow a mixing engineer to attend a session remotely. This has required a lot of research and experimentation, but I am confident that I will be able to deliver a reliable and efficient solution that will enhance the studio's capabilities.

The idea of remote access for audio production has been gaining popularity in recent years, especially with the pandemic forcing many studios to adapt to remote work. Being able to work with mixing engineers from anywhere in the world opens up new possibilities for collaboration and creative input, ultimately leading to better results for our clients. I am excited to be a part of this innovative solution and look forward to seeing it in action at Kingdomwork Studio.
